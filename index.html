How FeebaMeter works

From feedback capture to delighted customers in four simple steps.

1. Create survey

Pick a ready‑made template or start fresh. Add your brand in minutes.

2. Share & collect

Share QR at checkout or send links via WhatsApp/SMS. Watch responses roll in.

3. Reply instantly

Owner gets alerts for low ratings. Use quick replies to resolve issues.

4. Reward & grow

Offer points/coupons. Ask happy customers to review you on Google.

Frequently asked questions

Is there a free plan?

Yes. The Starter plan is free forever for 1 location with usage limits.

How do payments work?

We support subscriptions via Razorpay. You can pay monthly or annually. Invoices are emailed automatically.

Can customers earn rewards?

Yes. You can enable points and coupons tied to receipts. Configure thresholds inside the app.

Will it help Google reviews?

We nudge satisfied customers to post a public review after you've resolved their feedback privately.
